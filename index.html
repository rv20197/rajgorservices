About us

We are in this family business since 1993 as a dealer in Prince plumbing material along with this we are also in the business of folding and Adjustable props, support span, centering plate and all the other centering materials required for construction site on the rental basis as well as trading in the same.

We are well established iron and steel merchants in Thane City for past 70 years known by the name of Rajgor Lokhandwala family and since then we have been supplying all the required materials for the Construction Industry as we believe in the thing called as one stop shop. As we have a wide range of building materials such as Tor Steel, Round bars, Beam, etc. With the best in class quality. We are also into the service industry offering the Scaffolding and Centering Equipments on Rental Basis like H- Frames, Jack Props, Spans, Centering Plates, much more. As well as 30 years of experience in trading with the plumbing materials fulfilling all the demands and needs of our beloved customers providing them on time delivery of the best quality products with high strength and up to date feature such as high load bearing capacity, corrosion resistance and easy operation making the overall experience flawless.
